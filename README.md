# Unfunded Thoughts

A small place for notes about startups, ambition, work, and the strange pressure to build something of your own.

This is not meant to be polished advice from someone who has already figured it out. It is more of a public notebook for the thoughts people usually say in fragments: wanting to start up, wanting freedom, wanting meaning, wanting a story that feels bigger than a board full of tasks.

The writing here is interested in the gap between the dream and the work.

Why do so many people say they want to build a startup right now?

What are they actually asking for when they say it?

How much of it is money, status, freedom, boredom, taste, fear, or the simple need to feel like life is being authored instead of assigned?

Unfunded Thoughts is for that middle space.

Not anti-startup. Not startup propaganda.

Just trying to be honest about why the idea is so hard to shake.
